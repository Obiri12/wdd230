About the Accra Chamber of Commerce

Connecting businesses, empowering communities, and growing the economy—together.

Our Mission

The Accra Chamber of Commerce exists to support, promote, and empower local businesses of all sizes by offering networking, resources, and advocacy. We believe in growing a vibrant, sustainable economic environment through collaboration and innovation.

Our History

Founded in 1995, the Accra Chamber of Commerce has evolved from a small business collective to a thriving network of entrepreneurs, nonprofit leaders, and innovators. Over the years, we’ve hosted over 500 events, supported thousands of businesses, and been a voice for commerce in the capital region.

Our Core Values

  • Collaboration: We believe progress happens when we work together.
  • Integrity: We operate with honesty, trust, and transparency.
  • Innovation: We embrace fresh ideas and bold leadership.
  • Inclusivity: We champion diversity and equity in business.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and impactful business organization in Ghana, known for building sustainable local economies and thriving communities through strong partnerships and dynamic support services.
